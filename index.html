Trézór Bridge® revolutionizes how you move your crypto assets across blockchains. As part of the trusted TreZor™ Suite™ ecosystem, we provide enterprise-grade security with the simplicity you expect from industry-leading crypto solutions.

What is Trézór Bridge®? +

Trézór Bridge® is a secure cross-chain bridge solution that allows users to transfer crypto assets between different blockchains while maintaining the highest security standards of the TreZor™ Suite™. Our technology enables seamless interoperability between networks without compromising on safety or decentralization.

Which blockchains are supported? +

Trézór Bridge® currently supports Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon, Avalanche, and Solana, with more networks being added regularly. Our roadmap includes support for all major Layer 1 and Layer 2 solutions.

How does the security work? +

We utilize multi-signature technology combined with decentralized oracle networks to ensure all transactions are verified and secure. Your assets are never held in a single point of failure, and all smart contracts undergo rigorous third-party audits.

What are the transaction fees? +

Fees are calculated based on network conditions and typically range between 0.1% to 0.5% of the transaction amount. You'll always see the exact fee before confirming any transaction, with no hidden costs.
